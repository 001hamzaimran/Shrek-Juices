Our Story

From Founder Sophia Meadow

adventure awaits

For the past 2 decades, Sophia Meadow had a hidden talent. She had been juicing for personal health and vitality, quietly crafting her own nutritious concoctions in the comfort of her kitchen. Little did she know that this lifelong passion would eventually lead to the creation of something truly extraordinary

the sparks

In 2018, Sophia embarked on a life-changing trip to Aruba. While soaking in the sun and the island’s vibrant culture, she stumbled upon a humble juice bar that would forever alter the course of her life. What started as a daily visit to this juice haven soon sparked a revelation within her the realization that she had the potential to bring this vibrant, health-conscious experience to her own community.

MORE THAN A PIPE DREAM

On March 28, 2018, the seed of an idea took root in Sophia’s mind, and by May 1, 2018, Shrek Juice became a reality, opening its doors for the first time. Sophia’s passion was the driving force, but she knew that success required more than just enthusiasm. So, upon returning from Aruba, she delved headfirst into research, visiting countless juice bars in her area to gather insights and inspiration

MAKING MOVES

In January 2021, Shrek Juice embarked on a new chapter, relocating to a larger, more accommodating space. For 6 months, both locations operated side by side, but it soon became clear that focusing on the new location, which was just a mile away, made the most sense. So the decision was made to close the initial location and concentrate efforts on the new one

PRIMED TO THRIVE

As Shrek Juice Bar continued to flourish, Sophia recognized an incredible opportunity to share her passion for healthy living and nutritious creations with a broader audience. It was a pivotal moment when she decided to take the bold step of franchising the business. This decision was driven by a desire to empower like-minded entrepreneurs who share her vision of promoting wellness through fresh and delicious offerings. Through careful planning and a commitment to maintaining the high standards set by the original Maha Juice Bar, the franchise model was born, allowing the brand to expand its reach and touch the lives of even more individuals seeking a healthier, happier lifestyle.

Welcome to Shrek-Juices, where every sip is a journey through the whimsical world of flavors!
